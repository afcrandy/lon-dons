LonDons

Aberdeen FC fan club based in London

We’re the LonDons, an Aberdeen FC supporters club made up of more than 100 fans in the greater London area. Founded in 2011 by a group of likeminded Dons supporters, we organise meetups to watch matches, opportunities to socialise with other fans and events to raise money for charity.

We meet for all matches in pubs and organise events around central London, so have a look at our upcoming events schedule for our next meetup.

The LonDons are an inclusive group and family and friends are welcome. We are always looking for new members, so if you’re interested in joining, fill out the form below to get emails about our upcoming events, latest news and updates!
